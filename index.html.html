SONY

Sony entered the camera market with one vision: combine their world-leading electronics expertise with precision optics to build cameras that simply see more. The Alpha series, built on their proprietary Exmor R and Exmor RS back-illuminated sensors, captures light with a sensitivity and dynamic range that was impossible ten years ago.

Sony's Real-Time Eye AF was a revelation for portrait photographers, tracking subjects across the frame with uncanny precision — even with subjects wearing glasses or turning away from the camera. Their video capabilities rival dedicated cinema cameras at a fraction of the cost.

⚡
Exmor RS Stacked Sensor

Ultra-fast readout speeds that virtually eliminate rolling shutter distortion during video and high-speed shooting.

👁

Tracks humans, animals, birds, insects, vehicles, and aircraft with continuous, reliable autofocus lock.

Cinema-Grade Video

S-Log3 and S-Cinetone colour profiles deliver film-industry colour science directly in-camera.

CANON

For over 80 years, Canon has been synonymous with image quality that moves people. Their colour science — the warmth in skin tones, the accuracy of greens, the delicacy of highlights — has made Canon the default choice of portrait, wedding, and fashion photographers around the world.

The RF mount era represents Canon's most ambitious leap forward. The 54mm internal diameter allows lens designs with optical elements closer to the sensor than ever before, enabling faster apertures, better corner sharpness, and more dramatic rendering characteristics.

🎨
Canon Colour Science

Decades of R&D have produced a colour rendering engine that captures human skin tones with extraordinary warmth and accuracy.

🔁
Dual Pixel CMOS AF

Every pixel on Canon's sensor acts as a phase-detection AF point, creating buttery-smooth continuous focus for stills and video.

🏗
RF Mount Innovation

The world's widest mirrorless lens mount enables optical designs that were physically impossible on previous camera systems.
